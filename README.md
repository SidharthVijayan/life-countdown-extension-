# life-countdown-extension-
See your remaining life in real-time and track if your money goals match your time left.
⏳ Life is ticking. Literally.

This extension shows you exactly how much time you have left — in hours, minutes, and seconds — and connects it to your financial reality.

No fluff. No motivation quotes. Just truth, clarity, and a little humor.

---

💡 WHAT IT DOES

• Live Life Countdown  
Watch your remaining life tick down every second.

• Life Progress Bar  
See how much of your life is already used.

• Financial Reality Check  
Enter your goals and income — instantly see if you're on track.

• Income Gap Analysis  
Find out how much you SHOULD be earning vs what you ARE earning.

• Gamified Insights  
Get witty, honest, sometimes savage feedback that keeps you engaged.

• Daily Streak System  
Build consistency like a game — don’t break your streak.

---

This tool makes time visible.
